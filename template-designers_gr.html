ALEXANDROS FRANZOLINI

Alexandros Franzolini is an Italian industrial designer based in Milan. After completing his Bachelor's degree in architecture at the UEL University in London, Alexandros returned back in Italy to study Industrial Design, where he received a Masters from Marangoni Institute in Product Design, and a Masters in Strategic Design from the Politecnico di Milano. Since then, Alexandros has designed various products.

Italian Design by Alexandros Franzolini


SOTIRIS LAZOU

Sotiris Lazou, graduated from the technical school of Athens, in the field of interior design. A year later, he moved to Italy to study at the Domus Academy di Milano in Industrial Designs. Is a founding member of the Athens Secretariat of Industrial Design and professor at Athens School of Applied Arts A.K.T.O.  Since 1992 he manages his own studio. Currently realizes projects of Home, Contract & Office furniture, as well as urban furniture and household objects.



We strongly believe that every new project needs a fresh view and perspective. With this philosophy in mind, we have appointed famous industrial designers as A. Franzolini and S. Lazou. The result is unique and a showcase of a successful collaboration.
