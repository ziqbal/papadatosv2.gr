ALEXANDROS FRANZOLINI

Alexandros Franzolini is an Italian industrial designer based in Milan. After completing his Bachelor's degree in architecture at the UEL University in London, Alexandros returned back in Italy to study Industrial Design, where he received a Masters from Marangoni Institute in Product Design, and a Masters in Strategic Design from the Politecnico di Milano. Since then, Alexandros has designed various products.

Italian Design by Alexandros Franzolini




We strongly believe that every new project needs a fresh view and perspective. Papadatos company, having the confidence and self-assurance that years of experience ensure, we dare to experiment and try new things. We go beyond the ordinary and develop partnerships that open the way to new opportunities.

With this philosophy in mind, we have appointed Alexandro Franzolini to design a collection of new furniture. Believing in Alexandros' creative and fresh style, we combine his unique perspective with the values associated with Papadatos brand. The result is unique and a showcase of a successful collaboration.
